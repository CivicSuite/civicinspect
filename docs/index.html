CivicSuite / CivicInspect

Inspection drafts that keep inspectors in charge.

CivicInspect v0.1.1 ships a foundation for repeat-case lookup, inspector-owned report drafts, notice draft support, records-ready exports, and an accessible sample UI.

Shipping v0.1.1

What ships

  • Sample repeat-case lookup.
  • Inspector-owned report draft helper.
  • Notice draft helper with staff actions.
  • Records-ready export checklist.
  • Public sample UI at /civicinspect.

What does not ship

CivicInspect does not issue official findings, citations, fines, notices, inspection scheduling, legal advice, live photo analysis, live LLM calls, or system-of-record integrations.
